Things to note regarding the L'Oreal USA Bleed Premium:

  • If your publication does charge for bleed from an open rates perspective, we expect you to waive bleed charges for L'Oreal USA. If you agree, please enter 0% within the L'Oreal USA Bleed Premium section.
  • If your publication will not agree to waive bleed charges but instead reduce them, please enter the adjusted bleed premium percentage in the L'Oreal USA Bleed Premium section.
  • If your publication will not agree to waive or reduce bleed charges, then please ensure that the L'Oreal USA Bleed Premium that you enter matches the Open Bleed Premium.
